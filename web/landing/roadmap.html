Roadmap and implementation focus

Tonet roadmap tracks measurable quality gates: compatibility, stability, security, and user-facing delivery quality.

Near term

Expand landing and docs visibility, harden release delivery through Cloudflare CDN, and keep Servo integration quality checks repeatable.

Mid term

Grow HTML/CSS compatibility layers and improve browser shell ergonomics while preserving deterministic update and packaging paths.

Long term

Push deeper conformance and performance targets, broaden platform coverage, and keep all milestones documented with clear release notes.

Where to track details

The authoritative milestone document remains TONET_VISION.md. Public-facing summaries and use-case narratives live in the documentation portal.
